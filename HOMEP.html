Deal-it Cards


Deal-it Cards utilizes the drag and drop feature available on ios. It is unlike other card game apps in the aspect that you deal yourself the deck. So try it out, play what you want to play, deal how you want to deal, and enjoy the freedom of dealing out your own deck.
